# 🚀 SafeRide AI — Income Protection for Delivery Partners

> “When rain stops work, income shouldn’t stop.”

---

## 🌍 The Reality We’re Solving

Food delivery partners working with platforms like Swiggy and Zomato power India’s on-demand economy.

But their income has a hidden problem:

⚠️ If they don’t work → they don’t earn  
⚠️ If it rains → no deliveries  
⚠️ If pollution rises → unsafe to work  
⚠️ If curfew happens → zero income  

👉 A single disruption can reduce **20–30% of weekly earnings**

💔 And today, there is **NO financial safety net**

---

## 🎯 Our Vision

We built **SafeRide AI** — a smart, automated income protection system  
designed specifically for delivery partners.

👉 Not health insurance  
👉 Not vehicle insurance  

✅ **Pure Income Protection — when they need it most**

## ⚡ How It Works (Simple Flow)

1️⃣ Rider signs up in seconds  
2️⃣ Chooses a weekly protection plan  
3️⃣ AI continuously monitors real-world conditions  
4️⃣ Disruption detected (rain, AQI, heat, etc.)  
5️⃣ 💰 **Instant payout credited automatically**

👉 Zero effort. Zero stress.

---

## 📊 Real-Time Intelligence (Our Core Engine)

Claims are triggered automatically based on predefined conditions:
| Trigger Type | Condition | Impact |
|-------------|----------|--------|
| 🌧️ Rain | Rainfall > 50mm | No deliveries |
| 🌡️ Heat | Temperature > 40°C | Unsafe working conditions |
| 😷 Pollution | AQI > 300 | Health risk |
| 🚫 Curfew | Government restriction | No movement |
